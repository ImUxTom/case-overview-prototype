# Case model draft

## CASE

- A CASE must one or more DEFENDANTS
- A CASE can have one or more WITNESSES
- A CASE can have one or more VICTIMS
- A CASE has one or more DOCUMENTS

## DEFENDANT

- A DEFENDANT can have one or more CHARGES

## CHARGE

- A CHARGE has one or more POINT-TO-PROVE
- A CHARGE can have a SUBCHARGE
- A CHARGE has one or more DEFENDANTS
- A CHARGE can have one or more WITNESSES
- A CHARGE can have one or more VICTIMS
- A CHARGE has an ANTICIPATED PLEA

## POINT TO PROVE

- A POINT-TO-PROVE is proven by one or more pieces of EVIDENCE

## EVIDENCE

- A piece of EVIDENCE can be used to prove multiple points of a CHARGE
- A piece of EVIDENCE may relate to one or more VICTIMS
- A piece of EVIDENCE may relate to one or more WITNESSES
